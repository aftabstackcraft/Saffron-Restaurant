Welcome to our dining room

A restaurant built around spice, craft, and comfort.

Saffron & Sage brings regional Indian flavors into a contemporary dining room. Our kitchen roasts whole spices every morning, finishes breads in a live tandoor, and plates every course with fresh herbs, slow sauces, and thoughtful textures.

The experience is polished but never stiff: relaxed tables, attentive hosts, house-made pickles, handcrafted mocktails, and a menu designed for sharing.
